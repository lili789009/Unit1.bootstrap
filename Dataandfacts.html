1 million species of animals and plants face extinction

Animals are drastically exctincting

Unless greenhouse gas emissions are severely reduced, climate change could cause a quarter of land animals, birdlife and plants to become extinct. Distribution of animals is also affected; with many species moving closer to the poles as a response to the rise in global temperatures.

If we countinue like this

Global warming is projected to commit over one/third of the Earth's animal and plant species to extinction by 2050 if current greenhouse gas emissions trajectories continue as they are doing now there is going to be a catastrophic loss that would reduce biodiversity and alter both ecosystems and human societies across the globe.
